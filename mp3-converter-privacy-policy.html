1. Introduction

This App works entirely locally on your device and does NOT collect, store, or share your personal information.

This Privacy Policy describes how MP3 Converter: Video to Audio handles your information when you use our Android application.

2. Information We Collect

No Data Collected

We do not collect any personal information. Specifically, the App does NOT collect:

  • Your name, email address, or any account information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers (IMEI, serial number, advertising ID)
  • Contacts or phone book data
  • Camera or microphone data
  • Browsing history or usage analytics
  • Crash reports sent to external servers
  • Any data transmitted to our servers

All media conversion is performed entirely on your device using the FFmpeg library. No files, metadata, or user data are uploaded to any server.

3. Permissions Used

The App requests the following device permissions solely to provide its core functionality:

READ_MEDIA_VIDEO / READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE

To access video files you select for conversion.

READ_MEDIA_AUDIO / READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE

To access audio files you select for conversion.

WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE

To save converted output files to your device storage. (Used on Android 9 and below only.)

INTERNET / ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE

Used to check network connectivity only. No personal data is transmitted over the internet.

POST_NOTIFICATIONS

To show conversion progress and completion notifications.

FOREGROUND_SERVICE / FOREGROUND_SERVICE_DATA_SYNC

To keep conversions running reliably while the App is in the background.

WAKE_LOCK

To prevent the device from sleeping during active file conversion.

No permission is used to collect, track, or share your data.

4. Third-Party Libraries

The App uses the following open-source libraries solely to provide functionality. None collect personal data:

  • FFmpeg (mobile-ffmpeg) - Media file conversion engine
  • Glide - Image loading for file thumbnails
  • Lottie - UI animations
  • AndroidX / Jetpack libraries - Standard Android components

The App does NOT include:

  • Advertising SDKs (AdMob, Facebook Ads, etc.)
  • Analytics SDKs (Firebase, Mixpanel, etc.)
  • Crash reporting services (Crashlytics, Sentry, etc.)

5. Data Storage

All files processed by the App are stored only on your device. We do not upload, sync, or back up your files to any remote server or cloud service.

Converted files are saved to your device local storage. You can delete them at any time using your device file manager.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. Since the App collects no personal data, there is no risk of inadvertent collection from children.

7. Data Security

Because we do not collect or transmit any personal data, there is no personal data at risk of breach or unauthorized access. All file processing happens locally on your device, protected by your device own security model.

8. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share any user information with third parties because we do not collect any user information.

10. Your Rights
